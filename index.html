Survival Controls

  • W Move Forward
  • S Move Backward
  • ←/→ Turn Camera
  • F Shoot Zombies

Survival Rules

  • Kill ALL ZOMBIES to win the game
  • 5 NEW ZOMBIES spawn every 15 seconds
  • Collect ALL 20 GEMS to stop zombie spawns
  • ZOMBIE CONTACT = GAME OVER
